# Privacy Policy — Wall Mosaic

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

Wall Mosaic is designed to help you plan picture wall layouts using real-world dimensions. Your privacy is important, and this app is built to minimize data collection.

---

## Information We Collect

**Wall Mosaic does not collect, store, or transmit personal information.**

- No account is required
- No personal data is requested
- No analytics or tracking is performed
- No data is sent to external servers

All data you create in the app remains **on your device only**.

---

## Photos and Files

Wall Mosaic may access photos or files **only when you choose to import them**.

Used for:
- Wall photos
- Picture calibration images

These files are:
- Used **only within the app**
- **Not uploaded, shared, or transmitted**

---

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device, including:

- Walls and dimensions
- Picture records
- Layouts and placements
- Generated reports

You are in full control of this data.

---

## Third-Party Services

Wall Mosaic does **not** use:

- Analytics services
- Advertising SDKs
- Third-party data collection tools

---

## Data Sharing

Wall Mosaic does **not share any data** with third parties.

---

## Internet Connectivity

The app does not require an internet connection for core functionality.
